Query G: 

Brands in one category (Women) sorted by how many items they have + the brand's average price:

This query shows which brands have the largest Men assortment and their average price, enabling smarter ranking, 
filters, and brand curation. It also informs pricing and promotions by revealing typical price points per brand, 
improving user expectations and conversion.


PREFIX schema: <https://schema.org/> 
PREFIX vinted: <http://semanticweb2025.group5/vinted/>
PREFIX xsd:    <http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema#>

SELECT ?brand_name (COUNT(?item) AS ?num_items) (AVG(xsd:decimal(?item_price)) AS ?avg_price)
WHERE {
  ?item vinted:primaryCategory vinted:Women ;
        schema:brand ?brand ;
        schema:price ?item_price .
  ?brand schema:name ?brand_name .
}
GROUP BY ?brand_name
ORDER BY DESC(?num_items) ?avg_price
LIMIT 10
